IRB Timeline

  • Preparation
  • Application
  • Study Conduct
  • Closeout

Certification & Training

Complete CITI Training

Complete Institutional Research Conduct Ethics Training

Certify HIPAA Training is current for all research team members

Confirm eligibility of principal investigators at institution and/or site(s)

Study Design Specification

Determine and specify primary/secondary outcomes

Specify methodology

Select population

Estimate sample/population size

DSMP/DSMB

Determine if Data Safety Monitoring is required

Put together Data Safety Monitoring Board (DSMB)

Articulate study protocols for Data Safety in a Plan (DSMP)

Legal and Site Approvals

Review relevant federal and state statutes

Obtain approvals for human subjects research at all sites

Detail information sharing and privacy understanding with all sites

IRB Selection

Determine whether research is eligible for exemption

Identify institutions participating in research that also have applicable HHS assurances

Find appropriate IRB Board for research discipline

Selection and Recruitment Plans

Identify pool of potential participants for research study

Describe justification, if applicable, for participation of vulnerable persons

Review considerations for vulnerable populations, deception, ability to consent

Submit plan for equitable selection and recruitment of participants

Submit plan for ensuring informed consent and HIPAA Authorization

Submit informed consent and privacy authorization form templates

Compensation Plan

Disclose existing and potential conflicts of interest that may arise

Describe plan for compensating research participants

Articulate study protocols for Data Safety in a Plan (DSMP)

Data Collection Instrumentation

Submit copies of templates for data collection and survey instruments

Advertisement and Marketing Copy Creation

Submit templates of marketing and recruitment copy for study

Planning Protocol Changes

Determine scope of protocol change

Submit application for review of protocol change to IRB

Reporting Violations/Noncompliance

Identify scope of noncompliance according to institutional policy

Detail information about noncompliance, including dates, corrective actions, harm incurred

Implement IRB mandated response

Emergency Protocol Deviations

Submit detailed report of situation within 5 days of the action

Data Safety Monitoring and Audits

Provide schedule for regular review by DSMB

Continuing Review

Inform IRB of potential updates to study design or protocol

Submit application for continuing review

Protocol Review

Submit, if required, protocol for protocol review

IRB Closeout Notification

Submit notification of study closeout to IRB

Disclose emergent financial conflicts of interest

Inform all study sites and research collaborators of study closeout

Regulatory File and Documentation Audit

Ensure all regulatory documents are available

Conduct final audit of regulatory files

Select population

Estimate sample/population size

Source Data Verification

Ensure all data queries are resolved

Verify corrections, notes, and submission of all case report forms

Confirm patient medical records are available for review

Data Retention and Storage

Enumerate requirements for data storage compliant with 21 CFR Part 11

Arrange transfer and storage of data
